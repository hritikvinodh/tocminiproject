Our Story

Passion on Every Plate

✦

Founded in 2009 by Chef Arjun Menon, Savoria began as a humble passion project in a small kitchen in Fort Kochi. Today it stands as one of Kerala's most celebrated fine-dining destinations, beloved for its inventive fusion of coastal Malabar cuisine with modern European technique.

Every dish tells a story — sourced from local fishermen, nearby farms, and spice merchants who share our commitment to freshness and sustainability.
