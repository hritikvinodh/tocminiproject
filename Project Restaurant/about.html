About Savoria

Our Journey

From Passion to Legacy

Savoria was born from a simple conviction: that Kerala's extraordinary culinary tradition deserved a stage that matched its ambition. What began as a fifteen-seat bistro in 2009 has grown into a landmark destination with fifty covers, a dedicated bakery, and a private dining room.

2009
The Beginning

Chef Arjun Menon opens Savoria in a narrow shophouse on Princess Street, Fort Kochi, with a 15-seat dining room and a 6-item menu.

2012
First Recognition

Featured in Kerala Tourism's "Best Emerging Restaurants" list. The team doubles and moves to the current Marine Drive location.

2017
The Expansion

Savoria Bakery opens next door. Private dining room added for events, corporate dinners and bespoke celebrations.

2023
Award & Recognition

Kerala Tourism Award for Best Fine Dining. Chef Arjun named one of India's Top 50 Chefs by Culinary Arts Magazine.

2025
Today & Beyond

50 covers, a tasting menu, and a rooftop bar launching this year. The same passion — amplified.
