Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? +
Absolutely. We cater to vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other dietary needs. Please mention your requirements when making a reservation or inform your server at arrival. Our kitchen is happy to adapt dishes where possible.
Is there a dress code? +
We maintain a smart casual dress code. We ask that guests avoid beachwear, flip-flops, or sportswear. For Chef's Table experiences, we recommend semi-formal attire.
Can I bring my own cake? +
Yes, you may bring an external cake for celebration occasions. A plating and corkage fee of ₹300 applies. Alternatively, our pastry team can create a custom cake — just contact us at least 48 hours in advance.
Do you offer gift vouchers? +
Yes! Savoria gift vouchers are available in denominations of ₹500, ₹1,000, ₹2,500 and ₹5,000. They can be purchased at the restaurant or by calling us during opening hours. Valid for 12 months from date of purchase.
Is parking available? +
Complimentary valet parking is available on weekends (6pm–11pm). On weekdays, street parking is available along Marine Drive, and a public car park is a 3-minute walk away on Rajaji Road.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Reservations can be cancelled or modified up to 2 hours before your booking time without any charge. For Chef's Table bookings, a 24-hour notice is required. No-shows may be charged a ₹500 per person cancellation fee.
